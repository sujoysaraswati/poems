All together:

On the month of March 2010,
Four children were born in one pen.
Out of Shamik, Shrihan, Annika, and Vikas,
Annika is the one who likes dance.
Shrihan the tallest,
Shamik the oldest.
Vikas is in many sports teams,
like basketball, to say the least.
The doctor exclaimed, This is amazing!
Four young children destined for winning!
-By Shamik
















Shrihan's poem:

Karate, football, badminton,
Knitting, recording, education!
All these are interests of Shrihan,
Who was born on 22nd March!
His dad plays an important role-
Make sure that he's never bored!
He does farming in school-
That sounds very cool!
He studies in Advaya Shaale,
And is a champ in karate!
-By Shamik























Vikas's poem

Some time before Annika's birth,
It was Vikas's dawning turn!
Hi hobbies are soccer, basketball, athletics,
dance and gymnastics!
A little short, but that's OK,
As he's growing day by day!
He is happy and brilliant,
And is omniscient!
And as a star student,
He's very prudent!
-By Shamik






















Annika's poem

Hi! Hyaa! Hyaa! Hu!
Annika's learning Kalaripayattu!
Born on 29th March,
She loves dance!
Gymnastics is good,
But not too good.
Learning philosophy and the Gita,
In the kids' class of shloka!
She likes to be called Annie,
And she's her mom's sweet little honey!
-By Shamik
